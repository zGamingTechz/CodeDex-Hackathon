Why you should not marry me

  • I'm 6'1" – I can reach the top shelf without needing a ladder or a small child to stand on.
  • I'm smarter than a calculator, but I promise I won’t solve all your problems – that’s what Google is for.
  • I’m as quiet as a ninja – you’ll wonder if I’m there at all. Don’t worry though, I’m just processing deep thoughts... like "What’s for dinner?"
  • I’m the world’s best listener. You could talk about your sock collection for hours, and I’ll nod like it’s the most interesting thing ever.
  • Like a mystery novel – I’m full of plot twists. Spoiler alert: I still don’t know what I’m doing half the time.
  • When we argue, I’ll be the type to say, "You’re right, I’m wrong,” and then proceed to win the argument with a really well-timed meme. Love means never being wrong... and also always having memes on hand.
  • I’ll love you even if you steal the last fry. Just don’t make it a habit, or we might need relationship counseling.
